Smart Bin

A smart bin is an intelligent waste management system designed to enhance the efficiency and convenience of waste disposal. It utilizes technology, such as sensors and connectivity features, to automate and optimize waste collection and recycling processes.

Track collecting trucks

Tracking garbage collecting trucks involves using technology to monitor and manage waste collection vehicles. By equipping these trucks with GPS devices and other sensors, waste management companies can keep track of their location, activities, and performance in real-time.
